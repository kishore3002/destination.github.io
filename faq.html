FAQs - Indoor Navigation Mall

How do I use the indoor navigation system?

To use our indoor navigation system, simply visit our website and enter the name of the store or facility you want to navigate to in the search bar. Our system will generate the optimal route for you.

Is the indoor navigation system accurate?

Yes, our indoor navigation system utilizes advanced positioning technology to provide accurate navigation guidance within the mall.

Can I use the indoor navigation system on my mobile device?

Yes, our website is mobile-friendly, allowing you to use the indoor navigation system on your smartphone or tablet.

Are there any additional features available?

Yes, in addition to navigation, our website may offer features such as store directories, promotions, and event notifications.
